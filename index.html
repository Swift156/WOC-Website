Data Privacy Notice

Warriors of Christ is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy notice explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website.

What data do we collect?

We may collect:

  • Your name, email, and phone number when you fill in membership or contact forms.
  • Usage data (e.g., pages visited, time spent) through cookies and analytics tools.
  • Any information you voluntarily share in testimonies, prayer requests, or enquiries.

How do we use your data?

We use your data to:

  • Respond to your enquiries and prayer requests.
  • Add you to our membership database (with your consent).
  • Improve our website and understand how visitors engage with our content.
  • Share ministry updates and resources (if you opt in).

Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Contact us at privacy@warriorsofchrist.org for any requests.

Third‑party services

We use trusted third‑party services (e.g., website analytics, email platforms) that may process your data under their own privacy policies. We only share what is necessary.

By using this website, you agree to this privacy notice.

Our Mission

We are building a generation of young people who stand firmly rooted in faith, grounded in truth, and driven to share the gospel. Warriors don't wait for war to come to them — they go.

Our Vision

From local neighbourhoods to nations across the globe, God's Kingdom doesn't stop at borders — and neither do we. We are committed to seeing His light transform every sphere of society.

Discipline & Devotion

Weekly
Monday Fasting
Dry fast from 6:00 AM to 6:00 PM every Monday. We deny the flesh to feed the spirit — not as a burden, but as a chosen discipline that sharpens the warrior.

Optional
First Three Days Fast
The first three days of every month. Choose your fast: 6AM–6PM, liquid only, or dry. Every level of sacrifice is honoured. Come as you are, give what you have.

Night Watch Prayer
